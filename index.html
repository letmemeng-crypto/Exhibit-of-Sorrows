Exhibit of Sorrows: Can You Survive the Circus Museum?

Step into Exhibit of Sorrows, an unsettling point-and-click horror trip where childhood whimsy curdles into dread.

In Exhibit of Sorrows you wander a circus-themed museum filled with “cute” attractions that don’t stay cute for long. Every Exhibit of Sorrows display asks you to follow directions, push your luck, and accept that curiosity has consequences. The rules are simple: keep moving, keep thinking, and never forget the clown is watching.

Meet the Dolls in Exhibit of Sorrows

The corridors of Exhibit of Sorrows are lined with interactive dolls—each a small puzzle box that can reward you with a key or punish you for a careless choice:

  • Mr. Floaty: A buoyant smile, a sinking feeling, and a lesson you won’t unlearn.
  • Mr. Washy: Clean hands, dirty secrets—do what you’re told, not what feels safe.
  • Ms. Stretch: Find out how far a “harmless” request can go before it breaks you.
  • Mr. Handy: All fingers and thumbs, ready to help… or to take.

Why You Should Play Exhibit of Sorrows

Exhibit of Sorrows leans on psychological tension instead of cheap jump scares. As you progress, familiar shapes twist into something wrong, and your decisions can reshape what you see next.

Unsettling atmosphere: Circus colors, gentle music, and creeping dread that tightens scene by scene.

Meaningful choices: Exhibit of Sorrows dares you to be cruel, curious, or cautious—and shows the cost.

Free HTML5 gameplay: Play instantly on desktop or mobile, with no download or install.

How to Play Exhibit of Sorrows

Interaction: Use your mouse or touchscreen to tap, drag, and trigger the exhibits.

Objective: Follow each instruction carefully to reveal hidden keys and new rooms in the museum.

The golden rule: Whatever you do… DON’T UPSET THE CLOWN.
